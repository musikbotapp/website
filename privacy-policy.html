Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MusikBot collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Discord user ID
  • Server (guild) ID
  • Playback activity (tracks, artists, timestamps)
  • Usage statistics for features

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide music playback functionality
  • Generate listening profiles and stats
  • Improve performance and features
  • Prevent abuse and misuse

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only retained as long as necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. User Control

  • Users may access, or delete their personal data at any time by using the /manage-profile command directly within the bot interface

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of MusikBot means you accept any changes.

8. Contact

For questions or requests, join our support server.
